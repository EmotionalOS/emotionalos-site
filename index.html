Physiology-first sequence

The 7-Second Downshift

A compact reset protocol you can run in real time, in the middle of a board meeting, incident call or hard 1:1 – without disappearing to “regulate”.

  • Notice: micro-signals of hijack (heart rate spike, tunnel vision, heat, jaw/hand tension).
  • Interrupt: precise exhale-led breathing + small physical shift to hit the vagal brake.
  • Re-aim: one deliberate micro-action (question, pause, reframe) that keeps you in leadership mode.

Based on autonomic regulation, vagal tone, and executive function research – applied in a way operators can actually use under load.

The Emotional Capability Loop

You don’t need to “fix your emotions”. You need a tight loop that makes you more capable every time pressure hits.

  • Signal → What did your body do first?
  • Downshift → Run the 7-second reset.
  • Clarity → Reassess the decision / conversation.
  • Response → Choose a smaller, better-aimed move.
  • Reps → Log the rep. Load goes up over time. Capability compounds.
